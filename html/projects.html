Mandarin Stroke Order Generator
WebApp Dev

2024/dec/

This website helped students quickly search for Mandarin Chinese characters and stroke orders through typing them. Writing in Mandarin Chinese is notoriously difficult to master because of the vast amount of unique characters; when studying for exams, my classmates and I often had to search through textbooks, packets, and Google Searches to find out or remember how to write them. This project allowed us to streamline our studying, needing only to type the character into the site and see a visual brushstroke breakdown. This project was endorsed by my teacher and used by many Chinese students across all course levels.

Interlink Data Transportation System
Robotics & CV

2024/mar/

I developed the Interlink System to support video duplication from multiple cameras and their transportation to different machines on board an FRC competition robot using C and GStreamer. This system allowed our robot to process the same video feed differently on machines optimized to process them; this included object detection on some machines and mapping/match logging on others.
